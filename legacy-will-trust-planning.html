Will & Trust Planning

Turn asset distribution intentions into arrangements your family can understand.

We help clients record their wishes, appoint executors, consider beneficiary arrangements, and discuss trust structures where appropriate so assets can be managed and distributed according to the intended outcome.

For clients who want family and asset arrangements planned in advance. Discuss this service

What this may cover

  • Will preparation covering executor, beneficiaries, specific gifts, and residuary estate arrangements.
  • Distribution planning for young children, dependants, or family members who may need long-term support.
  • Trust planning direction based on family relationships, asset types, and the client’s intended outcome.

How we think about it

Beginning with the end in mind means asking what the client wants to happen in the end: immediate distribution, staged support, protection for a specific family member, or reduced conflict. Different outcomes call for different structures.
